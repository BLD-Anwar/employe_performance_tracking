Frequently Asked Questions

How is my Performance Score calculated? expand_more
Your score = Crushing 40% + Recovery 20% + Meetings 20% + KCC 20%. Each category is benchmarked against the regional average. Scores above 80% are considered excellent.
Why can I only see my own data? expand_more
The AgriPulse portal enforces strict data privacy. As a Field Operations user, you can only view your own KPIs, submissions, and reports. Managers and Staff can view team-level analytics.
How do I download my performance reports? expand_more
Go to My Reports and click the Download button (PDF, CSV, or Excel). Reports are generated instantly with your personal data.
My GPS location is not syncing — what should I do? expand_more
Ensure you have enabled Location Permission for your browser, and that you are within DGPS signal range. If the issue persists, submit a ticket with category "GPS Sync Failure".
